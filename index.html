About Us

Welcome to Kids Play Hall, your child's ultimate destination for fun, learning, and growth in Jaipur! We provide a safe, vibrant, and stimulating environment where children can explore, discover, and express themselves through playful activities.

Our mission is to foster creativity, social skills, and physical development in every child, ensuring they have an unforgettable experience filled with joy and laughter. We believe in learning through play, offering a variety of engaging activities designed to spark curiosity and imagination.

Admission

Age Groups

We welcome children from **1.5 to 8 years old**, with specific programs tailored for different developmental stages.

Admission Process

Simple 3-step process: **Enquire, Visit, Enroll!** Fill out our inquiry form, schedule a visit to our facility, and complete the registration paperwork.

Fees & Packages

Flexible packages available: **Hourly, Daily, Monthly, and Annual memberships.** Contact us for detailed fee structures and current offers.

Documents Required

Copy of birth certificate, Aadhar card (parent & child), and passport-sized photographs.
